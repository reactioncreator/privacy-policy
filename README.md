Privacy Policy for Reaction Creator
Effective Date: 5/1/2026
 Developer: Phantom Code
 Contact: reactioncreatorteam@gmail.com
Reaction Creator is a mobile application that allows users to create, preview, record, and export reaction videos directly on their device.
This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, how it is stored, and what choices you have when using Reaction Creator.
1. Information We Collect
Reaction Creator may collect or process the following types of information.
Account Information
If you sign in with Google, we may collect:
Your email address
Your Google account identifier or Firebase user ID
Your sign-in status
Your account plan status, such as Free, Premium, or Premium Annual
We use this information to manage your account, identify your plan, apply daily export limits, and provide access to premium features.
App Usage and Plan Information
We may collect limited app-related information, including:
Daily export count
Last export date
Subscription or plan status
Feature access status
App settings related to account access or export limits
This information is used to enforce free and premium limits, manage access to app features, and keep your account status accurate.
Video, Camera, Microphone, and Media Data
Reaction Creator uses your device camera, microphone, and selected media files so you can create reaction videos.
Your recorded videos, imported videos, previews, and exported videos are processed locally on your device. Exported videos stay on your phone unless you choose to share, upload, delete, or move them using your device or another app.
Phantom Code does not upload your created videos to its own server.
Diagnostics, Crash, and Analytics Data
Reaction Creator uses Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics to help understand app performance, diagnose issues, and improve stability. Firebase Crashlytics is a crash reporting service used to help developers understand when and why app crashes occur.
This data may include:
App version
Device type
Operating system version
Crash logs
Diagnostic information
General usage events
Performance-related information
We use this information to fix bugs, improve app reliability, and understand how users interact with the app.
Remote Configuration Data
Reaction Creator uses Firebase Remote Config to help manage app behavior, such as export limits or feature settings, without requiring an app update. Firebase Remote Config allows developers to change app behavior and appearance remotely while also using in-app default values.
2. Permissions We Use
Reaction Creator may request the following permissions.
Camera
Used to record your reaction video.
Microphone
Used to record audio while capturing your reaction video.
Photos, Videos, or Media Access
Used when you choose to import a video or select media from your device.
Storage or Download Access
Used to save exported videos to your device, depending on your Android version and device settings.
Reaction Creator only uses these permissions to provide the app’s video creation, recording, preview, and export features.
3. How We Use Your Information
We use the information collected or processed by Reaction Creator to:
Allow users to sign in with Google
Manage Free, Premium, and Premium Annual access
Apply daily export limits
Enable premium-only features
Save and display account status
Process videos locally on the device
Save exported videos to the device
Improve app performance and stability
Diagnose crashes and technical issues
Analyze basic app usage
Show ads to Free plan users
Support billing, subscription, and purchase-related functionality
4. Payments and Subscriptions
Reaction Creator may offer Premium and Premium Annual plans through Google Play Billing.
Google Play Billing is used to sell digital products and subscriptions inside Android apps. Payment processing is handled by Google Play. Phantom Code does not directly collect or store your full payment card number, bank account details, or other sensitive payment credentials.
We may receive purchase status information from Google Play to determine whether your account should receive Premium or Premium Annual access.
5. Advertising
The Free plan may show advertisements.
Ads may be provided by third-party advertising services. These services may collect or use certain information, such as device identifiers, ad interactions, approximate location, app activity, or other data, depending on the ad provider and your device settings.
Advertising is used to support the Free version of Reaction Creator.
6. Firebase and Google Services
Reaction Creator uses Firebase services, including Firebase Authentication, Firestore, Firebase Analytics, Firebase Crashlytics, and Firebase Remote Config. Firebase provides security and privacy information for its services, including data protection and processing details.
Firebase may process data such as account identifiers, app events, diagnostics, crash reports, configuration data, and other information needed to provide Firebase services.
7. How Your Videos Are Stored
Reaction Creator is designed so that your created and exported videos remain local to your device.
We do not store your reaction videos, imported videos, or exported videos on Phantom Code servers.
You control your exported videos through your device. If you share a video with another app, upload it to a platform, back it up to cloud storage, or send it to another person, that action is controlled by you and may be subject to the privacy policy of the service you use.
8. How We Share Information
We do not sell your personal information.
We may share or process limited information with service providers used to operate the app, including:
Google Firebase, for authentication, analytics, crash reporting, remote configuration, and database storage
Google Play Billing, for purchases and subscription management
Advertising providers, for displaying ads to Free plan users
We may also disclose information if required by law, legal process, or to protect the rights, safety, or security of users, Phantom Code, or others.
9. Data Retention
We keep account and plan-related information for as long as needed to provide the app, manage your account, enforce export limits, support purchases, resolve disputes, and comply with legal obligations.
Locally exported videos remain on your device until you delete them.
Crash, analytics, and diagnostic data may be retained by Firebase or related Google services according to their own retention practices and settings.
