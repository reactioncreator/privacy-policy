# Privacy Policy – Reaction Creator

**Effective Date:** 5/1/2026  
**Developer:** Phantom Code  
**Contact:** reactioncreatorteam@gmail.com  

---

Reaction Creator is a mobile application that allows users to create, preview, record, and export reaction videos directly on their device.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, how it is stored, and what choices you have when using Reaction Creator.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

Reaction Creator may collect or process the following types of information.

### Account Information
If you sign in with Google, we may collect:
- Your email address  
- Your Google account identifier or Firebase user ID  
- Your sign-in status  
- Your account plan status (Free, Premium, Premium Annual)  

We use this information to manage your account, apply limits, and provide premium features.

### App Usage and Plan Information
We may collect:
- Daily export count  
- Last export date  
- Subscription or plan status  
- Feature access status  
- App settings related to limits  

### Video, Camera, Microphone, and Media Data
Reaction Creator uses your device camera, microphone, and media files to create videos.

All videos are processed locally on your device. We do not upload your videos to our servers.

## Diagnostics, Crash, and Analytics Data

We use Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics.

This may include:

We use Firebase Remote Config to adjust app behavior (like export limits) without updates.
